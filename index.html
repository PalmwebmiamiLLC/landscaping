Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I have my HVAC system serviced?

+

We recommend having your HVAC system serviced twice a year - once in spring for your cooling system and once in fall for your heating system. Regular maintenance helps prevent breakdowns and extends equipment life.

What are signs that my HVAC system needs repair?

+

Common signs include unusual noises, poor air flow, inconsistent temperatures, high energy bills, and frequent cycling on and off. If you notice any of these issues, contact us for an inspection.

Do you offer emergency HVAC services?

+

Yes, we provide 24/7 emergency HVAC repair services. We understand that heating and cooling emergencies can happen at any time, so we're available around the clock to help.

How long does HVAC installation take?

+

Installation time varies depending on the system type and complexity. Most residential installations take 1-3 days. We'll provide a detailed timeline during your consultation.

What warranties do you offer?

+

We offer comprehensive warranties on all our work and equipment. Labor warranties typically range from 1-2 years, while equipment warranties vary by manufacturer (usually 5-10 years).
